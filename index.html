Our Medicines

Aspirin

Description: Aspirin is used to reduce pain, fever, or inflammation.

Benefits: Relieves pain, reduces fever, and decreases inflammation.

Disease Used For: Headaches, muscle pain, arthritis, and heart attacks.

Paracetamol

Description: Paracetamol is used to relieve mild to moderate pain and reduce fever.

Benefits: Effective for pain relief and fever reduction.

Disease Used For: Headaches, muscle aches, arthritis, backaches, toothaches, colds, and fevers.
